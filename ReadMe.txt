M. Alfarraj, Y. Alaudah, and G. AlRegib , "Content-adaptive Non-parametric
Texture Similarity Measure," 2016 IEEE Workshop on Multimedia Signal 
Processing (MMSP 2016), Montreal, Canada,  Sep. 21-23, 2016
